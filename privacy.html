Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 30, 2026

Etsy Profit Clarity respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Etsy Profit Clarity website.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Etsy Profit Clarity does not require you to create an account.

We do not collect, receive, upload, or store:

  • The contents of your Etsy statement files
  • Your Etsy transaction information
  • Your sales information
  • Your customer information
  • Your product costs
  • Your order information
  • Your calculated profits
  • Payment information

Your Etsy files are processed locally within your web browser. They are not uploaded to or stored on a server controlled by Etsy Profit Clarity.

2. Information Stored in Your Browser

The website may use your browser’s local storage to remember cost settings you enter, including:

  • Default product costs
  • Shipping costs
  • Packaging costs
  • Other costs
  • Fixed shop costs
  • Product-specific cost rules
  • Order-specific cost adjustments

This information remains on your device and is not sent to Etsy Profit Clarity.

You can remove this information by using the Reset costs option on the website or by clearing your browser’s stored site data.

3. Email Communications

The only way to contact Etsy Profit Clarity is by email.

When you email us, we may receive information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Your email address
  • Your name, when included
  • The contents of your message
  • Any files or attachments you choose to send

We use this information only to respond to your message, provide support, consider feedback, address problems, or maintain appropriate business records.

Email communications may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary for these purposes or as required by law.

Please do not email us Etsy customer information, financial records, or other sensitive personal information unless it is necessary for your support request.

4. Hosting and Third-Party Services

Etsy Profit Clarity is hosted through GitHub Pages. GitHub may automatically receive and process certain technical information when you access the website, such as:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser and device information
  • Operating system information
  • The date and time of your request
  • Pages or resources requested
  • Referring website information

This information is processed by GitHub under GitHub’s own privacy practices. Etsy Profit Clarity does not receive or control GitHub’s complete server logs.

The website may also load fonts, icons, scripts, or other website resources from third-party providers. When your browser requests those resources, the provider may automatically receive ordinary technical information, including your IP address and browser information.

Third-party services are governed by their own privacy policies and practices.

5. Analytics and Advertising

Etsy Profit Clarity does not currently use:

  • Google Analytics
  • Behavioral analytics tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Targeted advertising
  • Advertising cookies

We do not track users across unrelated websites or services.

We may introduce analytics or advertising in the future. Before doing so, we will update this Privacy Policy and provide any notice or consent mechanism required by applicable law.

6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Etsy Profit Clarity does not currently use its own advertising or analytics cookies.

The website uses browser local storage to save cost settings on your device. Local storage is not used to track you across other websites.

GitHub or other third-party resource providers may use cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies.

7. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers offer a “Do Not Track” setting.

Because Etsy Profit Clarity does not track users across third-party websites, the website does not currently change its behavior in response to Do Not Track signals.

We do not authorize third parties to collect information through Etsy Profit Clarity for cross-site behavioral advertising. However, hosting and resource providers may independently process technical information under their own privacy policies.

8. Sale or Sharing of Personal Information

Etsy Profit Clarity does not sell personal information.

We do not share the contents of your Etsy files, transaction records, cost information, or calculated results because that information is processed locally and is not received by us.

Information you voluntarily send by email may be processed by our email provider as necessary to deliver and store the communication.

We may disclose information received by email when reasonably necessary to:

  • Comply with applicable law or a valid legal request
  • Protect the rights, safety, or security of Etsy Profit Clarity or others
  • Investigate fraud, abuse, or unlawful activity
  • Obtain professional legal or technical assistance

9. Data Retention

We do not retain uploaded Etsy files or information processed locally in your browser.

Information stored through browser local storage remains on your device until you reset the costs, clear your browser data, or your browser removes it.

Email communications may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond, maintain records, resolve disputes, or comply with legal obligations.

10. Data Security

Because Etsy files are processed locally, Etsy Profit Clarity does not transmit or store their contents on a server controlled by us.

However, no website, device, browser, or method of electronic communication can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You are responsible for protecting your device, files, browser, and account credentials.

11. Your Choices

You may:

  • Stop using the website at any time
  • Remove locally stored costs using the Reset costs option
  • Clear the website’s data through your browser settings
  • Contact us regarding an email communication you previously sent

Because we do not receive or store your uploaded files or locally processed results, we cannot access, recover, correct, or delete those files or results on your behalf.

12. Age Requirement

You must be at least 18 years old to use Etsy Profit Clarity.

13. International Users

Etsy Profit Clarity is operated from the United States.

Third-party hosting and resource providers may process information in the United States or other countries. By using the website, you understand that technical information may be processed in countries with privacy laws that differ from those in your location.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the website, its features, or applicable requirements change.

When material changes are made, we will post the revised Privacy Policy on the website and update the effective date at the top of the policy. Your continued use of the website after a revised policy becomes effective means that you acknowledge the updated policy.
